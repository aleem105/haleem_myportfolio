FAQ

What do you need from me to get started?

I need WordPress login or sometimes need hosting login. And also need some more instruction. For example: – How many pages will be? – Send me logo if you have and color code for website. – If you have some content you can send me. – And finally you can send me reference website if you have.

Do I need to buy domain and hosting before?

Yes, you need to have a domain and hosting ready before we can start. I can recommend some reliable providers if you need assistance.

Can I change my text and images in future by own?

Yes, I will provide you with training on how to update content yourself, or you can opt for my maintenance services.

Will you support after work if there is any error?

Yes, I provide 30 days of free support after project completion for any issues that may arise.

How is your work process?

My process involves: 1. Requirement gathering 2. Planning and design 3. Development 4. Testing 5. Deployment 6. Training and support
